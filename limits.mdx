description: 'Limits for free and paid plans'
---

## Free tier

Your account starts out in free tier with 100 API requests per month

## Subscription

You can subscribe by clicking the "Buy Subscription" button at the top of the dasboard screen and paying $10. This payment automatically renews every month

A subscription authorizes you to make 1000 API requests per month 

## Counting Requests

One successful call to /page-text or /extract counts as one request. A failed request does not count for your usage

You can keep track of the number of request you have made in a month at the top right of the dashboard
